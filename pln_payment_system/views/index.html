About PLN

PLN (Perusahaan Listrik Negara) is the state electricity company of Indonesia. We are committed to providing reliable and sustainable energy to our customers. Our services include new connections, power adjustments, and temporary connections. We are continuously expanding and improving our services to meet the growing demand for electricity in Indonesia.
